# Startup Resources Hub

## Where to find support

### Join the community
- [Follow Startup Club Skopje for events involving startups and entrepreneurship](https://www.linkedin.com/company/startup-club-skopje/)
- [Follow ProCredit Bank for sponsored events like this one](https://www.linkedin.com/company/procredit-bank-macedonia/)
- [Follow SwissEP for events with experts from abroad and for help with entrepreneurship](https://www.linkedin.com/company/swiss-entrepreneurship-program/)
- [Follow Youth Entrepreneurial Service (YES) Foundation for business events](https://www.linkedin.com/company/yesfoundation/)

### Follow event hubs
- [Base42](https://www.linkedin.com/company/42-mk/)
- [Netaville](https://www.linkedin.com/company/netaville/)
- [Public Room](https://www.linkedin.com/company/public-room-skopje/)

### Reach out directly
- Reach out to founders on linkedin directly
- Reach out to domain experts in comments of posts you are interested
- Reach out to companies for research purposes and feedback

### Incubators and Accelerators
- [Startup Club Imam Ideja Incubator](https://startupclubskopje.com/mk/home/incubator/)
- [UKIM accelerator](https://bauaccelerator.com/)
- Regional accelerators and incubators
  - Search for balkan accelerators or incubators
- Global online incubator and accelerator programs
  - Search for global accelerators or incubators
  - There are many that have an online programs a well

### Investor help 
- [Follow Zephyr Angels for local business angel events](https://www.linkedin.com/company/zephyr-angels/)
- Go to pitching competitions and pithing events
  - You can look for regional and european pitching events. Applying is easy. Getting in is the hard part.
  - There are online pitching events as well if you don't have the means to travel
